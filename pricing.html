Important!

For the safety of all pets, we will only accept pets that have all up-to-date vaccinations, which includes kennel cough for dogs.
The Kennel Cough vaccination has to be completed 5 days before your dog first day for boarding.
These certificates must be produced by owners when leaving their pets with us for their holidays.
